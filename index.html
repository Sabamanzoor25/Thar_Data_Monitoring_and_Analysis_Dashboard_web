Nourishing Thar: A Sindh HEC Initiative to Tackle Malnutrition

Nourishing Thar

In Thar, IoT devices are employed to monitor malnutrition. These devices consist of sensors collecting real-time data on food, water, soil, and health indicators. Advanced analytics and machine learning analyze the data to identify patterns and risks. The insights obtained enable targeted interventions, resource allocation, and evidence-based policies to combat malnutrition effectively in Thar.
